2. Crosses and corrections

The boxes on the answer sheet are 5 millimetres wide. The boxes belonging to the same question are in one row. The number of the question is on the left hand side and the letter corresponding to the answer item is at the top of the column.

Use a black or blue pen to mark the fields. If you want to correct a mark, fill in the box completely. This field will then be interpreted like an empty box.
